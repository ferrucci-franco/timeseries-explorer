# Time Series Explorer publication

A technical publication describing Time Series Explorer, its design, and its
use for exploring Modelica simulation results is in preparation. It does not
have a public DOI or final bibliographic reference yet.

Until the publication is available, please cite the software itself:

> Ferrucci, Franco. *Time Series Explorer*. Version 0.1.0-beta.10. GitHub repository:
> https://github.com/ferrucci-franco/timeseries-explorer

The final title, authorship, venue, DOI, and recommended citation will be added
to this page and to `CITATION.cff` when they become available.
